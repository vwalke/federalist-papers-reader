# Federalist Reader X Avatar Design

## Decision

Use the selected **Pressmark F** direction for the Federalist Reader X account.

## Purpose

Create a profile image that is immediately recognizable at timeline size, survives X's circular crop, and feels native to the established Federalist Reader identity.

## Composition

- Square source canvas, designed as a circular emblem.
- Warm newsprint field extending to the edge.
- Two concentric ink rings, inset far enough to remain visible after X crops the image to a circle.
- Existing Federalist Reader Caslon “F” outline centered in oxblood.
- One small oxblood printer's diamond above and below the monogram.
- Generous clear space around the monogram.
- No wording, initials beyond the “F,” gradients, shadows, stains, distressing, or artificial aging.

## Brand Sources

- Monogram geometry: `public/app-icon.svg`
- Newsprint: `#e7deca`
- Ink: `#2a2722`
- Oxblood: `#672b28`

## Deliverables

- `public/profile/federalist-reader-x-avatar.svg`: editable vector master.
- `public/profile/federalist-reader-x-avatar.png`: 400×400 PNG for direct upload to X.
- PNG must remain below X's 2 MB profile-photo limit.

## Verification

- Confirm the PNG is exactly 400×400 pixels and uses RGB/RGBA color.
- Confirm the file is below 2 MB.
- Inspect at 400 px and at approximately 44 px.
- Inspect through a circular mask to confirm that both rings, diamonds, and the “F” remain balanced and legible.
- Confirm the vector master reuses the existing monogram path without distortion.
